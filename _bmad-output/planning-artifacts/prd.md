# PRD: Fanflare

## Executive Summary

Fanflare empowers Australian adult content agencies to scale from 5-50 models by unifying content creation, promotion, and monetization into a compliant, AI-assisted pipeline. Targets managers like Mia (boutique owner, 12 models, 40% time wasted on manual tasks) facing fragmentation across OnlyFans, tube sites, and websites—resulting in burnout, lost revenue, and compliance risks. Solves this with end-to-end automation from shoot booking to payouts, delivering 30-50% efficiency gains and 20-40% subscriber growth. While targeting managers, it empowers models and freelancers with transparent earnings dashboards, consent controls, and collaborative tools—fostering retention and trust.

### What Makes This Special

Unlike fragmented tools (e.g., OnlyFans basics or general CRMs), Fanflare embeds AU-specific compliance (R18+, ATO reporting) and anticipatory AI (posting suggestions with opt-ins) into every workflow, turning agencies into efficient "content-to-cash" machines. It includes offline-first resilience for remote/rural shoots and poor-signal locations, so the workflow never breaks on set. Core insight: In a multi-hundred-billion-dollar global market growing at ~8-9% CAGR (Transparency Market Research, 2025), proactive tech reduces risks while amplifying earnings—users choose it for ethical automation that feels supportive, not intrusive, with unfair advantages like proprietary AU datasets and rapid feedback loops.

## Project Classification

SaaS/Web App in Adult Entertainment domain; high complexity due to regulatory, privacy, and scalability demands; greenfield project building from scratch in an emerging AU market segment.

## Domain-Specific Requirements

### Compliance & Regulatory

- R18+ classification: Auto-classifiers for content at upload/booking, human override, audit logs for ACMA.
- Age verification: Integrate Yoti/ID.me for R18+ access, geo-locks for AU.
- Consent protections: Immutable digital forms at booking, revocation options.
- ATO taxation: Auto-track turnover, generate BAS-ready reports, payment summaries for models.
- GST thresholds: Flag $75K+ turnover, GST-free for exports.
- APPs privacy: Explicit consent for sensitive data, access/deletion rights, encrypted storage.
- Notifiable breaches: Auto-report within 30 days.
- AML/CTF: For crypto payouts over thresholds.

### Technical Constraints

- Security: End-to-end encryption for uploads/logs, role-based access.
- Privacy: Anonymized AI data, geo-locked AU storage, no unnecessary collection.
- Performance: Offline-first for rural users, real-time sync for urban.
- Availability: 99.5% uptime, disaster recovery for breaches.

### Integration Requirements

- OnlyFans API: Via third-party providers, no scraping.
- Payment processors: Adult-friendly like CCBill, Segpay; avoid mainstream like Stripe.
- Age verification: Yoti/ID.me integration.
- Data exports: ATO/GST compliant CSVs.

### Risk Mitigations

- Compliance breaches: Auto-flags reduce 95%, legal audits for AI accuracy.
- Payment blocks: Fallback to manual exports, diversify processors.
- Data leaks: Encryption, breach alerts, privacy-by-design.
- Regulatory fines: Proactive reporting, compliance training for users.

## SaaS/Web App Specific Requirements

### High-Level Summary
- **Core Focus**: Multi-tenant isolation, granular RBAC, tiered pricing, reliable integrations, and proactive compliance for AU adult agencies.
- **Key Metrics Tie-Ins**: Supports 99.5% uptime, 95% compliance accuracy, <5 min time-to-value (90% of users), and churn <10% via user-trust features and risk preventions.
- **Trade-Off Philosophy**: Balance scalability/security with MVP leanness (e.g., hybrid approaches prioritize safety without overhead).

### Technical Architecture Considerations
- **Tenant Model**: Full multi-tenancy with hybrid logical-physical isolation (tenant IDs + RLS in PostgreSQL, plus schema-per-tenant for high-sensitivity data)—balances security (prevents leaks) with scalability (supports 100+ agencies). Light MVP customizations (e.g., branding, timezones); opt-in sharing for collaborations. (Refined: 5-min onboarding wizard with quick-start templates, tested for <5 min completion in 90% of beta runs; stress-testing for 200+ agencies. See Risk Mitigations for load prevention.)

- **RBAC Matrix**: Granular RBAC/ABAC via CASL/Pundit, scoped to tenants with immutable audits. 
  | Role | Key Permissions | Notes |
  |------|-----------------|-------|
  | Agency Owner | Full access (users, billing, integrations) | Top-level control |
  | Agency Manager | Ops (bookings, content, payouts) | No deletion/billing changes |
  | Compliance Officer (optional) | Audits, exports | Sub-role under Manager |
  | Model/Performer | Self-scoped (profile, earnings, approvals) | One-tap revocation, privacy dashboard for access history |
  | Freelancer | Assigned bookings/payouts | Limited views |
  | Viewer | Read-only specifics | E.g., shoot details |
  | Platform (Internal) | Cross-tenant support | Super Admin/Support Agent |
  (Refined: User-visible ABAC overrides; ties to 95% accuracy via dynamic rules.)

- **Subscription Tiers**: Model-based plans with discounts, trials, and feedback-tied triggers/NPS for upsells (A/B tested for non-pushiness). Starter: Basics + offline tutorials; Pro: Full features; Enterprise: Advanced (API, SLAs). (Refined: Integrates with compliance scores for virality, supporting adoption metrics.)

- **Integration List**: OAuth-secured, webhook-driven with caching/redundancy for 99.5% uptime (multi-provider fallbacks). MVP: OnlyFans sync, Yoti/ID.me stubs, Segpay/CCBill (PCI DSS compliant). (Refined: 'Test connection' button, hybrid offline modes, in-app ratings; explainable opt-ins. See Risk Mitigations for outage prevention.)

- **Compliance Requirements**: Hybrid async checks with user-visible overrides/post-sync validation for 95%+ accuracy (on-device jobs, append-only logs). DPA templates (enforced onboarding), exports, audit trails, AU residency, breach detection (30-day notifications), auto-scoring (anonymized/actionable), auto-flags for regs (e.g., 2026 verification). (Refined: Human overrides, optional rural syncs; quarterly reviews. Ties to zero-audit-fail metrics; references APPs/WCAG for accessibility in dashboards.)

### Delivery Considerations (Merged Implementation + Risks)
- Prioritize modular code (feature flags) and database security for upgrades/audits.
- Test edges (rural syncs, high-volume) via beta pilots; ensure <5 min time-to-value with 'low-signal mode' toggle.

- **Integration Handling**: Webhook-driven with caching; rationale: Reduces load and enables real-time without the complexity of full event-sourcing.
