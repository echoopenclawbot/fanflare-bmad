# Product Brief: Australian Credit Score Checker

## Vision
A free, secure platform for Australians to check their credit scores and access reports, empowering better financial decisions through education and transparency, similar to Canstar's service.

## Problem Statement
Many Australians lack easy access to their credit information, leading to poor financial choices or surprises when applying for loans. Current options are limited, paid, or complex, leaving users without the tools to monitor and improve their credit health.

## Solution Overview
- **Free Credit Score Checks:** Users can check their score once per month after account creation.
- **Secure Identity Verification:** Use government records to verify identity (e.g., passport or driver's license + residential address).
- **Credit Report Access:** Provide summaries, contributing factors, and insights into credit history.
- **Educational Resources:** Guides on understanding and improving credit scores, with tips on responsible borrowing.
- **Credit Improvement Guidance:** Personalized recommendations and step-by-step plans for fixing bad credit ratings, including strategies like paying down debt, disputing errors, and building positive credit history.

## Key Features
- Account registration with email verification.
- Integration with major Australian credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, Illion).
- Dashboard for score tracking over time.
- Alerts for score changes or negative events.
- Personalized credit improvement plans with actionable recommendations (e.g., debt consolidation, budgeting tips, and links to financial counseling).
- Compliance with Australian privacy laws (e.g., Privacy Act, credit reporting codes).

## Target Audience
- Adults aged 18+ in Australia, including renters, first-time borrowers, and those managing debt.
- Focus on tech-savvy users seeking convenience, but accessible to all.

## Business Model
- Freemium: Basic checks free; premium features (e.g., detailed reports, monitoring alerts, advanced improvement tools) via subscription or partnerships.
- Revenue from affiliate links to financial products or data partnerships (with user consent).

## Success Metrics
- User acquisition and retention (e.g., monthly active users).
- Accuracy of credit data and user satisfaction.
- Compliance with regulations and low dispute rates.
- Improvement in user credit scores over time (tracked via follow-up checks).

## Risks & Mitigations
- Data security: Implement strong encryption, audits, and user consent.
- Regulatory compliance: Partner with legal experts for credit reporting laws.
- Competition: Differentiate with better UX and educational content, including unique credit improvement tools.
