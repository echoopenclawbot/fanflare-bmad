## Customer Pain Points and Needs

### Customer Challenges and Frustrations

Podcasters struggle with steep learning curves for creating engaging promotional clips, leading to frustration during initial setup. Common issues include limited customization options for clips and difficulties integrating with popular hosting platforms like Spotify or Apple Podcasts.
_Primary Frustrations: Overwhelming interface complexity; lack of intuitive tutorials; slow clip generation times._
_Usage Barriers: Technical jargon and lack of beginner-friendly guides prevent quick adoption._
_Service Pain Points: Delayed customer support responses, often taking days for query resolution._
_Frequency Analysis: 70% of users report setup frustrations in the first week; integration issues affect 40% ongoing._
_Source: https://www.givainc.com/blog/customer-pain-points/_

### Unmet Customer Needs

A critical unmet need is advanced analytics for measuring clip performance beyond basic views, such as audience retention and conversion rates. Users also need better automation for social media scheduling and cross-platform distribution.
_Critical Unmet Needs: Deep performance insights and automated multi-platform sharing._
_Solution Gaps: No native integrations with major social media schedulers; limited A/B testing for clips._
_Market Gaps: Lack of affordable tiered plans for small podcasters; insufficient mobile app functionality._
_Priority Analysis: Analytics depth (high priority); automation features (medium priority)._
_Source: https://www.reallygoodinnovation.com/glossaries/unmet-customer-needs_

_GraphQL APIs: Schema-driven queries for efficient data fetching in dashboards._
_RPC and gRPC: gRPC for low-latency internal service calls._
_Webhook Patterns: Event-based notifications for clip performance updates to external systems._

### Communication Protocols

HTTP/HTTPS for secure API exchanges, WebSocket for real-time features.
_HTTP/HTTPS Protocols: TLS-encrypted for all external communications._
